Quick answers

How long does a full wrap take?

–

Most full wraps take 4–6 days including the shoot. You get a delivery date at drop-off.

Will a wrap damage my paint?

+

No — premium cast vinyl is designed to protect paint when installed and removed correctly. We prep carefully and won't wrap compromised panels.

What does the video cost?

+

Transformation video is included with full wraps. Accent packages include a BTS reel. Photo/video-only shoots are booked via Roller Reels.

Can I book just a photo/video shoot?

+

Yes — book through Roller Reels at rollerreels.com for photo and video without a wrap.
