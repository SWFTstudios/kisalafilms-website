FAQ.

Straight answers about wraps, films, timing, and how to book.

Where are you based?

Jersey City, NJ. Appointment only — most days start in the garage.

Do you only wrap motorcycles?

Motorcycles are the focus. Reach out if you have a related machine project that fits the archive.

How long does a wrap take?

Depends on coverage and bodywork. Full sportbike color-changes are multi-day jobs. Partial accents can be faster. Photos help me quote accurately.

Can you film the transformation?

Yes — process films are part of what I do. Tell me in the inquiry if you want documentation.

How do I book?

Use the six-step inquiry on Work With Me. Most bookings finish on a phone call.

Is Kisala Films a production agency?

No. It’s a creator-led film and lifestyle platform. Commercial work is secondary to the films and Elombe’s story.

Where do I watch the films?

Start on Watch and the Series tape crate. Instagram carries day-to-day while longer cuts land.
