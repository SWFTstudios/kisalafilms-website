Who I am

Motorcycles. Automotive energy. Action sports. Adventure. Vinyl film and cinema — I’m chasing all of it, and connecting with people who feel the same wherever they come from.

Elombe Kisala

Kisala Films is a 2-in-1: film as vinyl wrap & paint protection film, and film as cinematic photos and videos. I’m based in Jersey City, wrapping bikes one set of hands at a time.

I don’t have a catalog of client wraps or YouTube episodes yet. Right now I’m wrapping my own bike a few times, documenting every step, posting the journey on Instagram, and getting good at the craft — wrapping, filming, photography — while I ride as many adventures as I can.

If you want your bike wrapped, you can book with me. Feature on the YouTube channel or this site is optional — only if you want it. Prices stay transparent either way.
