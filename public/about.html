LIFE THROUGH
MY LENS.

A world traveler with a camcorder — global, worldwide, always rolling.

I'm Elombe. I travel the world with a camcorder and turn what I find into film — automotive video magazines, cinematic brand edits, life vlogs, and cinematic travel pieces. Kisala Films is all of it in one archive.

By day I have a white-collar life — that story lives at elombekisala.com. This is the other reel: the roads, the machines, the people, and the places between departures.

Every glowing pin on the globe is somewhere I've pointed the camera. Kisala Films is a true film about a life in motion — recorded and directed by me.
