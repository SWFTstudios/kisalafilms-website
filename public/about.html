Kisala Films starts and ends with one perspective — mine. This brand exists to show life through my lens.

The person behind the brand

Camera in hand. Stories in motion.

I’m Elombe — creator, rider, and the focal point of Kisala Films. I document automotive culture, lifestyle moments, travel, and commercial behind-the-scenes with a cinematic eye.

This isn’t a corporate studio front. It’s a personal archive and living portfolio of where I’ve been, what I’m building, and how I see the world.
