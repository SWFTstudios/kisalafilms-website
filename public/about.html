Kisala Films is the true film of a life outside work — global, worldwide, behind the scenes.

I’m Elombe. By day I have a white-collar life — that story lives at elombekisala.com. Kisala Films is the other reel: the hours after, the weekends, the roads, the garage, the people, the builds.

I document motorcycles coming together, cars and meets, travel without a rigid plan, a one-man digital creative studio, and the skill sets I’m stacking in public. Cinematic vlogs. Life chapters. No agency template.
