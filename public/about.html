Craftsmanship & precision

The engineer's touch

Close-up, hands applying vinyl in the workshop

Elombe Kisala

Based in the heart of Jersey City, Elombe approaches motorcycle wrapping not just as an art, but as a precise engineering discipline. Every cut, every stretch, and every finish is calculated for maximum durability and visual impact.

Every project is also a story — the build gets filmed and cut into a transformation video, and the riders themselves often become episodes of their own on the K Films channel.
